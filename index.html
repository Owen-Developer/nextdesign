About Us
We’re Finley and Owen — two young entrepreneurs passionate about crafting sleek, modern websites that drive real results, and elevate brands online.

At NEXT DESIGN, we blend business expertise with cutting-edge web development to create websites that not only look great but help your business grow. Finley handles the client side with real-world business experience, while Owen leads development with a strong background in computer science and design. We started NEXT DESIGN to help small businesses and professionals build a powerful online presence — with clear communication, personal service, and results that speak for themselves.
